ALVIE

Learning observable models of Sancus enclaves and finding attacks through active automata learning.

Repository Paper Documentation

What it does

From specifications to witnesses

ALVIE combines TestDL attacker and enclave specifications with the L# learning algorithm. It learns models from the Sancus simulator, compares secret-dependent behaviors, and emits Graphviz witness graphs for observable violations.
